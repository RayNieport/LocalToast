Welcome to LocalToast

Feb 8, 2026 •
#Guide #Getting Started

Ingredients

  • 1 instance of LocalToast (Running)
  • 1 tasty recipe URL (from any major food site)
  • 0 Ads or Pop-ups
  • 0 Long-winded stories about my childhood
  • A pinch of Docker

Instructions

Step 1: Add a Recipe Click the + Add button in the top right. Paste a URL from a site like AllRecipes, FoodNetwork, or BBC Good Food. LocalToast will extract the title, image, ingredients, and instructions automatically.

Step 2: Review and Edit Before saving, you’ll see a preview. You can tweak the title, fix typo’s in the ingredients, or add your own tags (like “dinner” or “spicy”).

Step 3: Cook Open a recipe on your phone or tablet. You can set the screen to stay awake automatically while you cook (Kitchen Mode).

Step 4: Backup Your recipes are stored as simple files in the recipes/ folder. To backup your cookbook, just copy that folder to a safe place. You own your data.

Step 5: Enjoy That’s it! No accounts, no newsletters, just recipes.
